Assignment #1. Project website (10 points)

Deadline

12:00 12.02.2020

Description

Create and publish a static website describing the idea of your team project.

  • Use only English language in your UI and texts
  • Add project name and a short description on the index page
  • Provide a detailed description of your project (not less than 250 words) on any website page
  • Use (at least) 5 multimedia elements on your pages
  • Use any front-end component library to stylize your website (for instance, Bootstrap)
  • Add the "About" section with the (optional) name of your team and team members
  • Publish your website on any hosting platform (for instance, GitHub Pages)
  • Submit a link to your published website here

You can create a multi-page or a single-page website.

Other requirements

  • Use any online task management tool to track your team members collaboration (for instance, Trello). Make it public and provide a link in your submission
  • Use any version control system (VSC) and online service (GitHub/BitBucket/etc.) to host your code. Leave a link to your code somewhere on your website
  • Put a credit section on your website to thank your developers and collaborators.

You can ask for external help on any requirement, but do not forget to leave a credit.
